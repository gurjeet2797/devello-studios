## Contract Generation Instructions

When a client selects a consultation service, generate a detailed contract that includes:

### 1. Contract Header
- Service name and price
- Client information fields
- Date and contract number
- Service duration and location

### 2. Service Description
- Detailed description of the selected service
- Specific deliverables
- Timeline and milestones
- What's included vs. not included

### 3. Terms and Conditions
- Payment terms (due before service)
- Cancellation policy (24-hour notice required)
- Rescheduling policy (48-hour notice required)
- No-show policy (full payment forfeited)

### 4. Client Responsibilities
- Complete pre-consultation questionnaire
- Provide necessary business documents
- Attend session on time
- Implement recommendations as discussed

### 5. Consultant Responsibilities
- Deliver services as described
- Maintain confidentiality
- Provide follow-up support as specified
- Professional conduct throughout

### 6. Confidentiality Clause
- Mutual non-disclosure agreement
- Protection of business information
- Use of information for service delivery only

### 7. Limitation of Liability
- Services are advisory in nature
- No guarantee of specific business outcomes
- Client responsible for implementation decisions
- Maximum liability limited to service fee

### 8. Intellectual Property
- Consultant retains rights to methodologies
- Client owns business-specific recommendations
- No transfer of proprietary systems

### 9. Dispute Resolution
- Mediation before legal action
- Governing law and jurisdiction
- Professional conduct expectations

### 10. Signature Section
- Client signature and date
- Consultant signature and date
- Witness signature (if required)

## Additional Contract Elements

### Payment Terms
- Full payment required before service delivery
- Accepted payment methods

### Communication

- Service rescheduling options
- Refund considerations

## Usage Instructions

1. **Input Required:** Ask the client to select their desired consultation service
2. **Generate Contract:** Use the service-specific information above to create a detailed contract
3. **Customize:** Include client name, date, and any specific requirements
4. **Review:** Ensure all terms are clear and professional
5. **Format:** Present in a clean, professional document format
